==============
Privacy Policy
==============

1. This app does not gather personal information. Location data can always be disabled via Settings. A menu option is also provided to clear search history.

2. The developer does not know anything about the customer except what is given in the Google Wallet payment details. That data will not be used by the developer except for accounting and tax purposes, where required by the relevant tax authorities.

3. neutriNote does not, to the best of the developer's knowledge, send any data, anywhere.  The developer, however, has no control over the data which Google may collect when neutriNote uses Google services e.g. for some forms of location detection.

4. neutriNote is not part of any ad or statistics network. 

5. The developer has no interest in, and is morally opposed to, the selling or otherwise abuse of any customer data.
